The problem

Generic plans stop working the moment your body starts changing

Most nutrition apps hand you a static template built for an average person or doesn't adapt well. But as your body composition changes, your calorie needs, macro ratios, and portion sizes all shift.

SculptLab generates a batch prep plan from scratch based on your current body composition, then recalculates every time your stats update — so you're always eating to optimize your body towards a lean build.

Features

Built differently from every other meal planner

Macro-precise generation

Every plan is built to hit your specific protein, carb, and fat targets — calculated from your current TDEE and recomposition goals, not a generic template.

Food preference filtering

Tell us what you like, dislike, or can't eat. Your plan will never include something that doesn't fit — allergies, dislikes, and dietary styles all respected.

Adaptive recalibration

Log new measurements and your plan rebuilds automatically. No more guessing whether your old plan still applies to the body you have now.

Cook once, eat all week

One Sunday batch prep session — typically under 90 minutes — covers all your meals for the week. Mix and match components daily without cooking again.
